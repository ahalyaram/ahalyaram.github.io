A literature major’s love of words, stories and myths that mediate the world for us; a gratitudinous immigrant’s yearning to parse through obstreperous questions of my American society; a teacher’s zeal both for sharing and procuring knowledge; these are the tools I use to engage with the world.

I have had the good fortune to teach German language and literature at the University of Connecticut and Middlebury College.

After having traversed interesting verticals such as academia, HigherEd-LMS, IT and SaaS, I finally found my home in Salesforce as a Success Lead. With a motto of “ethics first, everything else next”, I marshal resources—Product Management, Support, Engineering, R&D, Platform & Custom Schema—to address customer issues. This results in clear accountability, consistent service and one face to the customer. The ability to understand key business issues and processes of my customers, almost like an insider of their organization, is a skill that I have masterred well.
